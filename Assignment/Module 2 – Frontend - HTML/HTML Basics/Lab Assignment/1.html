Navratri is a nine-night Hindu festival celebrated to honor the divine feminine energy. It is a time of devotion, dance, music, and vibrant cultural festivities across India.

Traditional Activities During Navratri

  1. Garba and Dandiya Raas dances
  2. Fasting and devotional prayers
  3. Decorating homes and temples
  4. Worship of Goddess Durga

Popular Foods

  • Sabudana Khichdi
  • Kuttu Ki Puri
  • Sweet Laddoos
  • Fruit-based dishes

Learn more about Navratri celebrations at Wikipedia.

Fun Fact

Navratri literally means "Nine Nights" and each day is dedicated to a different form of Goddess Durga, celebrating her victory over evil forces.
