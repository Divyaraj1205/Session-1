What is LAN,WAN,MAN ?

LAN (Local Area Network)
Definition: A LAN is a network that connects computers and devices within a limited area such as a home, school, or office building.
Types:
- Wired LAN (using Ethernet cables)
- Wireless LAN (using Wi-Fi)

WAN (Wide Area Network)
Definition: A WAN is a network that covers a broad area, such as a city, country, or even global connections. It connects multiple LANs.
Types:
- Public WAN (Internet)
- Private WAN (leased lines, MPLS)

MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)
Definition: A MAN is a network that spans a city or a large campus, connecting multiple LANs within a metropolitan area.
Types:
- Wired MAN (fiber optic cables)
- Wireless MAN (microwave, WiMAX)
